## Citation Format Conversion Guide

### APA to MLA
**APA:** Author, A. A. (Year). *Title*. Publisher.  
**MLA:** Author, First Name. *Title*. Publisher, Year.

### APA to Chicago
**APA:** Author, A. A. (Year). *Title*. Publisher.  
**Chicago:** Author, First Name. *Title*. Place: Publisher, Year.

### APA to Harvard
**APA:** Author, A. A. (Year). *Title*. Publisher.  
**Harvard:** Author Last Name, First Initial. (Year) *Title*, Publisher.
