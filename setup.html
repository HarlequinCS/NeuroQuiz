Welcome to NeuroQuiz™

How It Works

NeuroQuiz™ is a smart quiz that automatically adjusts to your skill level. Answer questions correctly, and it gets harder. Answer incorrectly, and it gets easier. It's like having a personal tutor that knows exactly how to challenge you!

How Difficulty Changes

  • Get it right? The next question will be a bit harder
  • Get it wrong? The next question will be a bit easier
  • Answer 7 in a row correctly? You'll jump to harder questions faster!
  • Struggling too much? You'll move to an easier level, but start at the hardest difficulty of that level

Special Bonus: Level Up Offer

If you answer 10 questions correctly in a row on the hardest difficulty, you'll get a special offer:

  • Keep playing at your current level
  • Move up to the next level (like going from Elementary to Secondary)

What Are Education Levels?

  • Elementary (Level 1): Easy questions for beginners
  • Secondary (Level 2): Medium questions for intermediate learners
  • University (Level 3): Hard questions for advanced learners

What Are Difficulty Levels?

  • Beginner: Start here if you're new to the topic
  • Intermediate: You know the basics and want more challenge
  • Expert: You're really good and want the toughest questions

What You'll See While Playing

  • Your streak - how many you got right in a row
  • Your points - harder questions give more points
  • Your current level - which level you're playing at
  • After finishing, you'll get a detailed performance report showing how well you did
